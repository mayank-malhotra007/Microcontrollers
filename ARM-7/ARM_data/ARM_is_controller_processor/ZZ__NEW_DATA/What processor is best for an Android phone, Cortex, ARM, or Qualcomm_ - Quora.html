First things first... RIP English.

ARM is an semiconductor fabrication company just like INTEL which specializes in making  low power consumption processors.

Cortex is a brand name under ARM Holdings.(just like I3,I5 etc)

Qualcomm doesn't make their own processors, they license the processors from ARM, make suitable changes to them, integrate their own technologies(Quick Charge)  and sell them under the SNAPDRAGON series.

The best processor for an Android right now would be Snapdragon 810 or Tegra K1 chip
